Our Mission

At Killer Keebs, our mission is to provide enthusiasts and professionals with high-quality, customizable keyboards and keyboard parts. We believe in the power of personalization and performance, and we strive to bring the best products to the market to enhance your typing experience.


Who We Are

Founded by a team of keyboard aficionados, Killer Keebs is dedicated to the art and science of mechanical keyboards. Our founders' passion for keyboards stems from a love of gaming, programming, and design, leading to the creation of a company that prioritizes quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.


What We Offer

  • Mechanical Keyboards: From entry-level to high-end, we offer a range of mechanical keyboards to suit every need and preference.
  • Custom Keycaps: Add a personal touch to your keyboard with our wide selection of keycaps in various designs, colors, and materials.
  • Switches: Find the perfect switch for your typing style, whether you prefer tactile, clicky, or linear switches.
  • Keyboard Accessories: Enhance your setup with our collection of cables, stabilizers, lubricants, and more.
  • DIY Kits: For those who love to build their own keyboards, we provide comprehensive kits with all the necessary components.
